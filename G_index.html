Tide

Handmade upon request. All prices include our custom wall mount.

All measurements exclude the wall mount. For total measurements add 10 cm to height and width.

Delivery: Currently it is only possible to pick up at our studio in Frederiksberg, Denmark. Contact us at hello@kibun.dk if you wish to purchase a panel but need it shipped. Because our panels and wall mounts are handcrafted upon order with great care for detail, production may take up to six weeks.

Care: Please note that the wool fabric is very sensitive to heat and humidity and can be damaged by water, ironing and steaming. For this reason it is also important to place the panels on a flat surface, and to not fold or crumble them.
